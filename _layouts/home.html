This is Refugee Tech


We build bridges between all parts of society to enable refugeesâ€™ potential, relying on efficiently designed and developed technology solutions.

In order to solve relevant problems that refugees face, we involve all the stakeholders with knowledge about the crises, including those who are trying to reach out to refugees as well as the refugees themselves.

Our method identifies, using methods backed by science and research, the most relevant and urgent problems that can have an innovative tech solution. The best ideas that result from our process get implemented into products that will be released and these products will improve life for thousands of people.

Our progress


  • 1. Problem identification

  • 2. Idea generation

  • 3. Hackathon

  • 4. Open source

  • 5. Product release

1. Problem identification

No single organization or person can know or understand what problems are prioritized, and if solved, will affect the most people. This type of analyzis can only be done through a joint effort. This is what this phase is about. Using scientifically based methods for data gathering, using a structured process, we convey as many relevant stakeholders as possible to understand, prioritize and do proper impact analysis to lay the groundwork for others to come up with sustainable solutions.

2. Idea generation

Sustainable solutions have multiperspective approaches and create positive feedback into society. This phase is about letting people and organizations create teams with different backgrounds that contribute to our understanding of potential solutions. It is an intense learning experience as great solutions demand deep understanding about the problems. The output of this phase will allow for techies, designers and engineers to create technology that implements those solutions.

3. Hackathon

Using an innovative approach to hackathons, we allow for the best solutions to become realized as prototypes. But this phase is much more than coding. Without proper resource commitments it would be impossible to develop prototypes into developed products. Our process is designed so that solutions with high potential will be developed and market tested through dedicated teams, in cooperation with relevant stakeholders, with proper funding.

4. Open source

Naturally Refugee Tech's products will be open source to allow for anyone to contribute to its development. But this phase is not only about tech, it is about creating the necessary foundation to release a successful product. Hopefully it could lead the way for others to apply similar solutions using our recipes for success(or failure).

5. Product release

This phase is where we make our final stand. We release the products and services created and make sure that other people and organizations could carry them on to make an even greater impact that improves the lives of thousands of people.
